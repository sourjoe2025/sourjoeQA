Beginner path

Start here with sourdough

Sourjoe starts new bakers with a simple learning path: understand the essentials, learn how starter and leaven work, follow the baker's process, then move into guided recipes and calculators.

The recommended order

New bakers should begin with Essentials, continue through Starter & Leaven, then use Baker's Process to understand the rhythm of a bake.

Book plus Recipes

The Book explains the ideas behind sourdough. Recipes turn those ideas into timed, practical bake sessions so you know what to do next rather than guessing from a loose formula.
