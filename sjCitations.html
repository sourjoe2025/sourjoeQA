Source-use policy for Sourjoe

How to use this page

  • Specific beats general. Prefer a named article, book record, paper, or guide page over a site homepage.
  • Recipes and calculators are guidance, not lab reports. Formula outputs should be treated as practical estimates unless a section explicitly says otherwise.
  • Weights beat volumes. Flour, water, salt, starter, and add-ins should be weighed whenever possible; volume conversions are best treated as kitchen approximations.
  • Food-safety and allergen claims need regulatory or medical-facing sources. Do not rely on a recipe blog for gluten-free, allergen, or nutrition-label claims.
