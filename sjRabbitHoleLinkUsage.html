sjRabbitHoleLink Usage

This page demonstrates a portable Rabbit Hole component modeled on the working Glossary behavior. It does not depend on sjLearnScripts.js or sjLearnStyles.css.

Keyboard support: tab to a term and press Enter or Space. Press Esc to close open Rabbit Holes.

1) Glossary-style envelope mode

This is the closest match to the current Glossary behavior: clickable inline term, one open at a time, grey veil, and the owning row becomes the Rabbit Hole envelope.

  • : The amount of water relative to flour, usually expressed as a percentage.

    In bread formulas, 75% hydration means 75 grams of water for every 100 grams of flour. Higher hydration often produces a more open crumb, but the dough also becomes harder to handle.

    This item demonstrates the “one envelope” effect: the term line and the deeper content below are framed as one Rabbit Hole.

  • : A preferment built from an active starter and fresh flour/water before mixing the final dough.

    Some bakers use starter for the mother culture and levain for the build prepared specifically for a bake.

    This demo intentionally mirrors the working Glossary interaction pattern rather than inventing a new one.

  • : The rapid rise in loaf volume during the first stage of baking.

    Good oven spring usually depends on fermentation strength, shaping tension, scoring, heat, and steam.

2) Plain linked Rabbit Hole mode

This mode keeps the trigger outside the Rabbit Hole card look. Each trigger simply opens a standalone panel.

— open a plain Rabbit Hole card below.

A plain mode panel is useful when you want a simple linked popup/card rather than a full envelope around the trigger row.

— this one can stay open at the same time because the group allows multiple open panels.

In this group, data-sjrhl-single="false" allows multiple open linked Rabbit Holes.

3) Public API examples

These buttons show the small public API. This is useful for gradual integration later.

Recommended markup pattern

<div class="sjrhl-group" data-sjrhl-group="example" data-sjrhl-single="true" data-sjrhl-overlay="true">
  <div class="sjrhl-item sjrhl-item--envelope" data-sjrhl-item>
    <button class="sjrhl-trigger" data-sjrhl-trigger data-sjrhl-target="panel-id">Clickable term</button>
    <div class="sjrhl-panel sjrhl-panel--flatten-in-envelope" data-sjrhl-panel id="panel-id" hidden>
      ... deeper content ...
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
